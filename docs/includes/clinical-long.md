## Publications, proposals, and positioning

To drive **publications**, **research collaborations**, and to establish **Marbles Health** as a subject matter expert in neuromodulation, we have undertaken many projects. My contributions started with researching the use of **tDCS in stroke** for the [**AIIMS NCAHT** proposal](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1j8Banu2QI7SylTzGCos4j91b74r-3rqt84r5gEmMUHY/edit?usp=sharing) — surfacing literature on tDCS across **neurological disorders**, motor parameters, and verification via **imaging** and assessments. That connected to work for **Drs. [Shabbari](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1xhSUaPaI1pEa5-hpQ6W9eRMNSvHxG_LATqlGXV5kbv8/edit?usp=sharing) and Sridhar** on **C3/C4** motor rehabilitation. It also led to **license requirements** and a draft mail for **[Sridhar](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1zG16IPP9kGwArj1RoS2xrjLF9moCVMApGUnr1tPCJrA/edit?usp=sharing)** promoting **EASE** as a **domestic**, readily available device versus imported systems — arguments that extend to other **Indian** academic institutions and hospitals.

## NIMHANS abstract

We submitted an abstract for **[NIMHANS](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1215mYxcWU4iGzJH99-RGPApUrTlNJNhkcUxKg3KxQ4I/edit?usp=sharing)** arguing that **frontal ERPs** in **Go/No-Go** and **Stroop** could be read with our headband; it was selected by the OC but we did not pursue it further — it remains a template for future research.
